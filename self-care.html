List of Crisis helplines

  • 24/7 Crisis Support Peel Provides intervention services to people who are in mental health or addiction crisis. Call 905-278-9036.

  • Access CAMH Providing centralized information, intake and scheduling for most of the Centre for Addiction and Mental Health (CAMH) services, Access CAMH makes it easy for patients, families, friends and healthcare professionals to find the help and services they need with a single phone call.

  • Across Boundaries Providing equitable, holistic mental health and addiction services for racialized communities.
