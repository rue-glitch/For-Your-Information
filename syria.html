#syrianwar

After more than 9 years of violent conflict in Syria, more than 5 million are in need of humanitarian aid. More than half the population has fled their homes, with 5.7 million refugees and 6.2 million people displaced. Over 2.1 million children are out of skill and have grown up surrounded by conflict, deprived of a safe and protected environment. By donating today, emergency and lifesaving support will be provided to Syrians as well as used to create education centers.
